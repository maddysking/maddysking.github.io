The Case of JonBenét Ramsey

The Case that Gripped the Nation

JonBenét Brief Biography
  • Born: August 6, 1990 in Atlanta, Georgia
  • Died: December 25, 1996 in Boulder, Colorado
  • Family: John and Patsy Ramsey(parents), Burke Ramsey(brother), John Andrew Ramsey(half-brother), Melinda Ramsey (half-sister), Elizabeth Ramsey(half-sister)
  • Pageant Titles: Little Miss Colorado, Little Miss Charlevoix, Colorado State All-Star Kids Cover Girl, America's Royale Miss, and National Tiny Miss Beauty
  • Resting Place: St. James Episcopal Cemetery in Marietta, Georgia

Case Overview

John and Patsy Ramsey, affluent residents of Boulder, Colorado, made media headlines across the United States after the murder of their six year old daughter JonBenét. JonBenét Patricia Ramsey became a household name following her untimely and gruesome death on the Christmas of 1996. The six year old beauty queen was found dead in the basement of her home after a presumed kidnapping; bound, gagged and strangled. It has been nearly twenty years since the discovery of JonBenét’s body; however, detectives are no closer to bringing the murderer to justice than they were in 1996. Although no one has ever been formally charged or indicted in the case, many who worked close to the case and amateur sleuths online argue over the potentiality of an intruder or the involvement of JonBenét’s mother, father, or nine year old brother, Burke.
